# Website Content: Aeflyt.dk

## Core Services
- **Erhvervsflytning (Business Moving)**: Focus on Zealand (Sjælland). "Flytning med kærlig hånd". Aim to make moves easy so businesses can get back to work quickly.
- **International flytning**: Moving abroad.
- **Opbevaring (Storage)**: Storage solutions.
- **Transport**: General transport services.

## Key Messaging
- "Din partner til erhvervsflytning" (Your partner for business moving)
- "Flytning med kærlig hånd" (Moving with a loving hand)
- Focus on reliability and efficiency.
