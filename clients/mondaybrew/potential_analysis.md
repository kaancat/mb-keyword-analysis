# Monday Brew - Strategic Analysis & Campaign Potential

## 1. Executive Summary

Monday Brew is a Copenhagen-based digital agency offering integrated customer acquisition systems for B2B service companies. With a conservative budget of 5.000-10.000 DKK/month and national Danish targeting, the campaign strategy must be highly focused on high-intent keywords with clear commercial value.

**Core Campaign Objective:** Generate qualified B2B leads seeking predictable customer acquisition systems.

**Strategic Approach:** Given the low budget and B2B niche, we recommend a phrase/exact-heavy match type strategy targeting high-intent commercial searches related to lead generation, digital bureau services, and marketing automation.

## 2. Website & Service Audit

### Strengths
- Clear value proposition: "Infrastructure over campaigns"
- Strong differentiation: Direct founder access, no handoffs
- Documented case studies with results
- Clean landing page structure per service

### Weaknesses for Ads
- Broad service offering makes tight ad grouping challenging
- Some service pages incomplete (CMS setup notes visible)
- No explicit pricing (common in B2B, but affects Quality Score)

## 3. Keyword Analysis Summary

### Primary Keyword Categories

| Category | Priority | Rationale |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Lead Generation | HIGH | Direct match to core offering "filled calendar" |
| Google Ads Bureau | HIGH | Specific service with commercial intent |
| Digital Bureau B2B | HIGH | Captures agency-seekers |
| Meta/Facebook Ads | MEDIUM | Service offered, but secondary |
| Marketing Automation | MEDIUM | Technical offering, longer sales cycle |
| CRM Implementation | MEDIUM | Supporting service |
| B2B Website | MEDIUM | Part of "Konvertering" offering |
| Problem-Aware (få flere kunder) | LOW | Informational, expensive |

### Match Type Strategy (Budget-Driven)

Given 5.000-10.000 DKK/month:
- **Exact Match (60%):** High-intent core terms
- **Phrase Match (35%):** Mid-volume modifiers
- **Broad Match (5%):** Discovery only if budget allows

Initial estimates (to be validated in Phase 3):

- "leadgenerering" - 100-300/month

- "digital bureau" - 300-700/month
- "crm implementering" - 50-150/month

## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|------------|------------|
| Low search volume | HIGH | Focus on phrase match, accept long-tail |
| High CPC in bureau/agency space | HIGH | Exact match protection, quality score focus |
| Brand unfamiliarity | HIGH | Focus on service terms, not brand |
| Long B2B sales cycle | MEDIUM | Track micro-conversions, attribution |
| Budget exhaustion mid-month | MEDIUM | Daily budget caps, dayparting |
