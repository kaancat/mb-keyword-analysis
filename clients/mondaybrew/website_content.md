## Key Observations for Keyword Strategy

1. **The overarching goal is "filled calendar"** - Focus on lead generation and predictable customer acquisition terms
2. **Not a traditional agency** - Position as systems builder, not campaign manager
3. **B2B service focus** - Exclude consumer/e-commerce keywords
4. **Low budget** - Must be extremely targeted, phrase/exact match dominant
5. **Holistic offering** - Keywords should capture both demand gen AND technical infrastructure needs
6. **Danish language** - All campaigns in Danish for Denmark market
